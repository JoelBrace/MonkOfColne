Flagging & Setts

Overview

Installing setts is a fundamental civil engineering activity, essential for creating durable and visually appealing paved surfaces. This process involves laying natural or manufactured stone blocks, known as setts, to construct reliable and attractive surfaces for various applications.

Setts are ideal for roadways, driveways, walkways, and public spaces. Their robust nature ensures they withstand heavy usage and environmental stresses, making them suitable for heritage projects, urban revitalization, and residential enhancements.

At Monk Of Colne, we prioritize meticulous craftsmanship, ensuring surfaces are precisely laid, accurately aligned, and securely sealed. Our highly trained professionals utilize specialized equipment and proven techniques, consistently delivering outstanding results.
