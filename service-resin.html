Resin Bonding

Overview

Resin bonding is a popular civil engineering technique known for creating durable, attractive, and practical surfaces suitable for various applications. This method involves meticulously hand-laying a carefully blended mix of aggregates bonded with high-quality resin, ensuring a robust and visually appealing finish.

At Monk Of Colne, we offer a diverse selection of stone mixes to suit every project's aesthetic and practical requirements. Certain colour choices may require UV-resistant (UVR stable) resin to prevent sunlight-induced colour fading after installation. Regardless of your project's scope or style, our extensive range of colours guarantees an ideal solution tailored precisely to your needs.

Our skilled professionals carefully hand-lay each resin-bonded surface, focusing on precision, consistency, and attention to detail. We emphasize quality craftsmanship, ensuring a beautifully finished surface that is both long-lasting and uniquely suited to your project's vision.
