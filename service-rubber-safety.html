Rubber Safety Surfacing

Overview

Rubber safety surfacing is an essential civil engineering solution widely recognized for creating safe, durable, and visually appealing surfaces. It involves meticulously hand-laying high-quality rubber materials, such as Wetpour and Rubber Mulch, providing a soft yet resilient surface ideal for numerous applications.

At Monk Of Colne, we offer an extensive range of colours for both Wetpour and Rubber Mulch surfacing, with the ability to blend these colours uniquely to suit any project's specific requirements. This versatility allows us to create bespoke surfaces tailored precisely to your needs, ensuring both functionality and aesthetic appeal.

Rubber safety surfacing is ideally suited for play areas, running tracks, trim trails, and various recreational environments. Our experienced professionals carefully hand-lay each project, prioritizing accuracy and detail, ensuring a consistent, safe, and visually pleasing finish.
