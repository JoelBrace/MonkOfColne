About Us

Established in 1963, we are a family‑run firm with a reputation for delivering work to a high standard on projects of every size.

Our History and Values

Founded as a family firm, Monk of Colne has grown steadily while maintaining a commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Our longevity in the industry is a testament to our dedication and the trust our clients place in us. We are proud recipients of the Borough of Pendle Quality Award for ‘Outstanding Contractor’, reflecting our ongoing pursuit of excellence.

We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern methods to deliver work that lasts — safely, efficiently, and with care for the communities we serve.
